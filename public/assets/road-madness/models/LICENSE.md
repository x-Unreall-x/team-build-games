# Kenney Car Kit

The `derby.glb` and `monster.glb` models are adapted selections from Kenney's
[Car Kit](https://www.kenney.nl/assets/car-kit), version 3.1. The shared
`Textures/colormap.png` is included unchanged so the original materials render.

The `derby-cyber.glb` and `monster-cyber.glb` models are local derivative
variants generated from those same CC0 meshes by `scripts/style-road-models.mjs`.
They keep the original chassis geometry, replace the materials with Road Madness
cyan/red cyber paint, and add simple bumper, armor, light, and underglow meshes.
